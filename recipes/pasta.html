Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons water, or as needed

Steps

  1. Step 1:Make the dough.
  2. Step 2:Turn the dough out and knead it for 10 minutes.
  3. Step 3:Let the dough rest.
  4. Step 4:Divide the dough into balls.
  5. Step 5:Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into desired shapes.
